# Stock Explorer: Top Feature Opportunities from Competitor Research (Rounds 20-27)

## Overview
This document summarizes the most promising feature opportunities identified from competitor research rounds 20-27. These represent gaps where competitors have implemented features that Stock Explorer lacks, validated by market demand and alignment with Stock Explorer's "historian, not stock picker" positioning.

## Methodology
Analyzed `docs/research/competitor_research.md` which contains competitive research rounds 7-27, focusing on:
- Feature Gap Summary sections from each round
- Recommendations sections highlighting immediate, short-term, and medium-term priorities
- Key Insights highlighting strategic opportunities
- Effort estimates and priority rankings (P1 = highest priority)

## Alignment with Product Vision
All recommended features reinforce the "historian, not stock picker" positioning:
- Focus on explaining what happened, not predicting what will happen
- Educational rather than advisory
- Contextual learning through narrative and storytelling
- Progressive disclosure and beginner-friendly design
- Integration of data with plain-language explanations

## Competitive Validation
Each opportunity is validated by 2-3 competitors demonstrating market demand:
- International platforms (Luca AI, Ticker.ai, Copilot Money, Datawallet, Spiking)
- TW platforms (群益, 財報狗, 元大證券, 永豐金證券, 玉山證券)
- Asian market leaders (Kabutan, Simply Wall St, Stockopedia, Morningstar)
- Education-first innovators (Zerodha Varsity, Khan Academy, Stash, Acorns, Planting.tw)
